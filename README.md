This website displays a travel agency that helps people plan their flights. It provides forms for users to fill.

Technologies:

   CSS    :9.2%
JavaScript:7.3%


How to use: 
Click on The sun icon in nav bar to enter dark mode;
Enter info in Flight reservation form and click on Book now btn to alert and display a payment form;
Click on pay now in form to alert;
Click on Book now in available flights to scroll to the flight reservation form and add the name of the country where you clicked the btn;
Same if you click on the buttons in the New year sale section;
Click on the images in Hot deals section to take you to website;
Slideshow to see Feedbacks;
Enter name in contact form and click on send to alert a message;
Click on a word from the first column of the footer to scroll to the section;
